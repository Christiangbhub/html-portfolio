My top 3 Best Movies of all Time

The Avater: Aang the last airbender

To be honest, this just came out a few weeks on Netflix, but it is the live-action of the animated series of Nickelodeon's The Avater.

The Hatful Eight

This has a lot of violence and action and a bit of mystery. Trust me you need to watch this.

The One Piece

One of my favorite animes to watch.This is about a young pirate who wants to find the hidden treasure known as the one piece.
